2024 Thalia Symposium: Cyber Crime

Get ready for the 2024 Thalia Symposium! On the 24th of april 2024, we'll be organising a day full of interesting talks about cyber crime. With talks from researchers, companies and even the Dutch Police, you'll definitely learn something new and interesting about the field of cyber crime.
We'll be holding the symposium in Theaterzaal C in the Elinor Ostrom building of the RU. Sign-ups will be opened on the 29th of march, on the thalia.nu events page.


Practical Details

The symposium will be from 10:00 to 17:00 with a borrel right after.
You can check the schedule using the bar on the left, where you can also take a look at which speakers will be attending and which companies will be attending the company market during the break!
Stay tuned for more information about our lineup of speakers, and companies in attendance of the market. Be sure to keep an eye on our website for updates and announcements leading up to the event.
